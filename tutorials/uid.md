## Step 1: Log in to the Vantage Website

Open the [Vantage](https://vantage.rip) website and log in with your account.

![Login Page Example](../assets/img/uid_1.png)

## Step 2: Locate the Account Card

After logging in, click on the account card in the top-right corner of the page.

![Account Card Example](https://via.placeholder.com/800x400?text=Account+Card)

## Step 3: Copy the UID

In the account card, find the UID field and click the **Copy Button** next to it to complete the operation.

![Copy UID Example](https://via.placeholder.com/800x400?text=Copy+UID+Button)
